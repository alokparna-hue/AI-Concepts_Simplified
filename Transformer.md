## What are Transformers?

Transformers, as the name says, transforms raw input data into structured output data. This transformation is enabled by understanding the context, sequences, and relationships between the sequences.

## How Does a Transformer Model Work?

_Transformer_ works on the basis of **attention**. This refers to the mechanism of _paying_ _attention_ to particular parts of the input data before generating any output. For example, **"The cat jumped over the fence to catch the mouse. But it ran away."** Here, the transformer will focus its _attentoion_ on the word **it**, which refers to the mouse and not the cat in this context.
