## Introduction

This document explains Large Language Models (LLMs) with examples.

## What are LLMs

LLMs are AI systems that are trained on **vast datasets**, which enable the models to interpret natural language and generate responses accordingly.

## Why are LLMs Important?

LLMs form the **core strength** and determine the **efficacy** of AI models. Powerful LLMs can understand complex user inputs, process huge chunks of data, retain context, and generate desired responses. It's important to select the **right LLM or a combination of LLMs** to address specific needs.
