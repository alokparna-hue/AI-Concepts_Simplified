## Introduction

AI has taken the world by a storm. Every organization wants to integrate AI into their processes and deliver results using it. However, amidst the jargons, the definitions remain in the foreground. This document focuses on the basic definitions of AI and Gen AI along with relevant examples.

## What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the technology that helps computer systems replicate human intelligence for speech and pattern recognition, visual perception, decision making, and so on.
